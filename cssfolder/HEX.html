CSS HEX Colors

A hexadecimal color is specified with: #RRGGBB, where the RR (red), GG (green) and BB (blue) hexadecimal integers specify the components of the color.

HEX values are specified using the format:

#RRGGBB

Where RR, GG, and BB are hexadecimal values between 00 and FF (same as decimal 0-255).

HEX Color Examples:

#FF0000

#0000FF

#00FF00

#800080

How HEX Works:

  • Red: #FF0000 - Maximum red, no green, no blue
  • Green: #00FF00 - No red, maximum green, no blue
  • Blue: #0000FF - No red, no green, maximum blue
  • Black: #000000 - No red, no green, no blue
  • White: #FFFFFF - Maximum red, green, and blue
